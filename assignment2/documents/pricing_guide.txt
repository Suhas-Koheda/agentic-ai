=========================================
SOFTWARE PRICING & PLANS GUIDE
=========================================

1. PRICING PLANS:

* Standard Plan:
  - Price: $10 per month (billed monthly) or $96 per year (billed annually - saves 20%).
  - Target: Solo developers, freelancers, and small projects.
  - Features included:
    - 1 User seat
    - Up to 5 projects
    - 10GB cloud storage
    - Standard email support (24-hour response time)
    - Basic integrations

* Professional Plan:
  - Price: $29 per month (billed monthly) or $276 per year (billed annually - saves 20%).
  - Target: Growing teams and professional businesses.
  - Features included:
    - Up to 10 User seats
    - Unlimited projects
    - 100GB cloud storage
    - Priority email and chat support (2-hour response time)
    - Advanced integrations (Slack, Jira, GitHub)
    - Basic analytics and reporting dashboard

* Enterprise Plan:
  - Price: $99 per month (billed monthly) or $950 per year (billed annually - saves 20%).
  - Target: Large organizations and high-scale operations.
  - Features included:
    - Unlimited User seats
    - Unlimited projects
    - 1TB cloud storage (expandable)
    - Dedicated Support Manager and 24/7 phone support
    - Custom integrations and API access
    - Advanced security (SAML SSO, Audit logs)
    - Custom contract and invoicing options

2. BILLING CYCLES:
- All plans are available in Monthly or Annual cycles.
- Annual cycles are billed upfront and come with a 20% discount compared to monthly pricing.
- Upgrades are processed immediately with prorated charges. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
