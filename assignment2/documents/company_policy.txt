=========================================
COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT - CUSTOMER SUPPORT
=========================================

1. REFUND POLICY:
- Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of their initial purchase or renewal.
- Refund requests after 14 days are generally not permitted unless under extenuating circumstances.
- All refund requests must be routed to Billing Support, flags set to Pending, and must wait for Supervisor/Management approval. No automated refunds are permitted.
- If approved, refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to the original payment method.

2. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION:
- Customers may cancel their subscription at any time.
- Cancellation requests do not yield immediate refunds unless requested within the 14-day refund window.
- All subscription cancellation requests must be verified, require Human-in-the-Loop approval to prevent accidental data loss and check for retention offers, and must be approved by a supervisor before finalizing.
- Upon cancellation, the subscription remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.

3. ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY:
- Customers can request permanent closure of their accounts.
- Account closure is irreversible and results in the permanent deletion of all customer data, files, and history after 30 days.
- Due to the destructive nature of this action, all account closure requests must be routed to Account Support, require Human-in-the-Loop approval, and must be verified by a supervisor.

4. COMPENSATION POLICY:
- Compensation or account credits may be offered to customers experiencing prolonged downtime or severe service degradation.
- Standard agents cannot issue compensation. All compensation requests are classified as high-priority and require Supervisor approval.
- Maximum standard supervisor compensation is limited to $50 or 1 month of free service. Higher amounts require executive manager approval.

5. ESCALATION TO MANAGEMENT:
- A customer may request their case be escalated to management if they are dissatisfied with the support resolution.
- Agents must document the reason for escalation, draft an escalation summary, and route the case for Human-in-the-Loop review.
- The supervisor must review the customer history and approve the escalation before transferring the ticket to the management queue.
